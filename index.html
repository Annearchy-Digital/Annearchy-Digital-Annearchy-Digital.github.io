Creative Technology

The world is built from new ideas. This campaign is your chance to support making more in the world. Over the last 7 years in the Tri-Cities Tech-Entrepreneurship ecosystem, I have run events for new ideas from 24-hour hackathons or whole weekend events, plus 4-weeks, 8-weeks, and 12-week business accelerator events.

Each of these learning programs has been targeted toward businesses that are either starting or looking for an inflection point. This has led to the launch of dozens of businesses in an ecosystem that has created hundreds of jobs. If I can do it for them here in Tri-Cities, I believe I can do it for you anywhere.

2020 was the year that everything changed; maybe 2021 can be the year things change for you. So many people put things on hold in this last year - delaying new careers to even leaving jobs over childcare. That means the world has even more big ideas waiting for an opportunity to spring forth. This could be your opportunity and I would like to take the plunge with you.

Founder & Futurist

My experience in startups means I know what it takes to take a product from zero to 8.5 million users and grow a company from zero to 55 million revenue. My experience with people means I know what it takes to lead through a time of crisis and pitch to a crowd of 2,000. Through it all, I believe I know how to keep moving forward when life tries to knock you down.

Erik holds a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Washington State University. He has over 18 years of education and experience in software including building enterprise apps for the US Department of Energy, international consulting, and making mobile tech for B2B and B2C startups. Most recently, he spent 5 years leading the US products team for the fastest growing tech company in Australia.
