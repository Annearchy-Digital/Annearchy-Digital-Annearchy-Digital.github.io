What is Annearchy Digital?

Annearchy Digital offers strategic and technical consulting from Erik Ralston. Erik has taken products from zero to 8.5 million users and companies from zero to 55 million revenue. Erik has over 18 years of education and experience in technology including international consulting, mobile tech for B2C & B2B startups, and most recently lead the US products team for the fastest growing tech company in Australia. Erik is a Microsoft technologist, public speaker, and has helped start ventures from SaaS companies to food trucks.

Spark Your Imagination

Man & Machine Learning

"Are you trying to make the robots takeover or save us from the robots?"

This question was the simple driver for my TEDx talk "Man & Machine Learning", which explored the choices we're already giving machines and how we need to seeking to upgrade the software in our fellow humans rather than fearing what's running in our computers.

Featuring an AI-powered rendition of The Trolley Problem, the talk was delivered at TEDxRichland in 2018 and echoes into the present as a warning that in many ways the machines have already taken over and it's up to us to open our eyes and adapt to the inevitable - as we always have before.

Why Annearchy?

Annearchy Digital was named for my three daughters - each of which have the middle name Anne. In this family, we believe in Caring, Sharing, and Learning, which are the best parts of what it means to be human. When considering projects, I will only take them if they make a better world for my daughters.
